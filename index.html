Information
  • This Scene-1 provide information of count of New Cases of Covid-19 vs Date. Bar Chart was being used in this Scene Showing New Cases of Covid-19 as Y-Axis and Date as X-Axis.
  • User can hover over the bar chart which display tooltip showing count of New Cases and Date information.
  • Highest Number of New Cases Covid-19 was recorded on 16th July 2020.
  • Next Scene(Scene-2) will shown count of Deaths because of Covid-19 vs Date.
  • This Scene-2 provide information of count of Deaths because Covid-19 vs Date. Bar Chart was being used in this Scene Showing count of Deaths as Y-Axis and Date as X-Axis.
  • User can hover over the bar chart which display tooltip showing count of Deaths and Date information.
  • Highest Number of Deaths because of Covid-19 was recorded on 14th April 2020.
  • Next Scene will give user flexibility to drill down further on Covid 19 New Cases/Deaths per State.
  • This Scene(Scene-3) will give user flexibility to drill down further on Covid 19 New Cases/Deaths per State.Use can select "State" from drop down and can further select "Death" or "New Cases" from radio option.
  • Bar Chart was being used in this Scene Showing count of Deaths or NewCases as Y-Axis and Date as X-Axis per selected State.
  • User can hover over the bar chart which display tooltip showing count of Deaths/count of New Cases and Date information.
